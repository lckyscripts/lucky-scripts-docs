# Restaurant/Business Management

> 💰 Paid FiveM script for restaurant and business management

## 📋 Overview

A complete business management system for restaurants and shops:

- **Business creation** - Setup multiple businesses
- **Employee management** - Hire and manage staff
- **Order system** - Process customer orders
- **Financial tracking** - Monitor profits and losses

## ⚙️ Requirements

- ESX Legacy or QB-Core
- ox_lib for menus
- ox_inventory (recommended)

## 📥 Installation

1. Purchase from [Tebex Store](https://lucky-scripts.tebex.io/)
2. Download the resource
3. Configure `config.lua`
4. Add to server.cfg

## ⚡ Features

### Business Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Restaurant | Food preparation and service |
| Bar | Drinks and entertainment |
| Shop | Retail merchandise |
| Service | Professional services |

### Management Commands

```
/business menu - Open management panel
/biz hire [player] - Hire employee
/biz fire [player] - Fire employee
/biz info - View business stats
```
