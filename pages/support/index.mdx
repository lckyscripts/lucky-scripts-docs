## ❓ FAQ

### Installation Issues

**Q: The script won't start**
> Make sure you have added `ensure [resource-name]` to your server.cfg and all dependencies are installed.

**Q: Dependencies missing errors**
> Run `refresh` in your server console, then restart the resource.

### Configuration

**Q: How do I configure the scripts?**
> Each script comes with a `config.lua` file. Read the comments carefully and adjust values as needed.

**Q: Can I use this with other frameworks?**
> Our scripts support both ESX and QB-Core. Check the documentation for framework-specific setup.

### Purchasing

**Q: How do I purchase paid scripts?**
> Visit our [Tebex Store](https://lucky-scripts.tebex.io/) for all purchases.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
> Due to the nature of digital products, we generally don't offer refunds. Contact us if you have issues.
